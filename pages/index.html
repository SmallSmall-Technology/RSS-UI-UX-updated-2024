Frequently asked questions

Get answers about renting with RentSmallsmall

RentsmallSmall is a platform that offers customers access to flexible payment plans on homes. Payment plans include monthly, bimonthly, biannual and quarterly. We do not collect agency or agreement fees.

We also provide property owners with premium services that include providing verified subscribers (tenants) for their property, saving them time and stress in getting tenants. With us, you earn your rental income as and when due without suffering any payment defaults.

No, RentSmallsmall does not pay rent on behalf of tenants, nor do we offer a repayment plan for rent. Each tenant is fully responsible for making their own rent payments as outlined in the subscription agreement.

If you have any further questions about our services or policies, please don't hesitate to contact us at 0903 633 9800

When you find the unit you like, you fill out the inspection request form when you click the inspection button, you get a confirmation email and our agents get in touch with you within 24-48 hours. If you see the unit and like it, you apply for verification, if successful, you make payments, get your agreement and move in.

At Rent SmallSmall, We do not charge for inspection, no matter how many properties you desire to inspect!
