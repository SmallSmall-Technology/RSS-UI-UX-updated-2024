Getting started

Make the switch and start living smart

With our easy-to-use service, you can choose between virtual and physical property inspections to fit your schedule. Simply create an account to access our properties and select your preferred date and time for a personalised tour. Our team is here to ensure your inspection experience is seamless and informative, all inspections are free!

We verify users to ensure a seamless rental experience and foster trust between you and us, which is why you will need to verify your renter profile to live in our property. As part of the process, you will be required to submit proof of income, employment details, a valid ID, and information for a guarantor. This information will help enhance your credibility and improve your chances of securing your desired rental.

Rent, move-in and living

Home sweet home

Lock in your new home by passing the verification process, making your first rent payment, and signing the agreement. These essential steps demonstrate your commitment and pave the way for a hassle-free move into your new space.

Once you've secured your rental, it's time to move into your new home! We'll prepare for your arrival by ensuring the space is cleaned and ready for you. Additionally, we'll provide you with the contact details of the facility managers and other important information about the property. While you’ll take care of the setup, we’ll make sure everything is in order, so you can focus on the excitement of settling into your new space and making it truly your own.

As you settle in and enjoy your new rental, we appreciate your commitment to being a responsible tenant. Timely rent payments and open communication help us maintain a positive relationship, ensuring that your living experience is as enjoyable and stress-free as possible.

Renewing or ending your lease

Plan for your future with our easy renewal and termination process

Stay ahead of the game—renew your rental subscription effortlessly by email before it expires, ensuring continuous access to your home without any interruptions!

Deciding to end your rental subscription is easy—simply notify us via email before your current term ends, and we'll handle the rest, ensuring a smooth transition for your next steps.
