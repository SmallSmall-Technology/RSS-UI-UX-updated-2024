Frequently asked questions

Get answers about renting with RentSmallsmall

What is about RentSmallsmall?

RentSmallsmall is a platform that offers customers access to flexible payment plans on homes. Payment plans include monthly, bimonthly, biannual, and quarterly. We do not collect agency or agreement fees.

We also provide property owners with premium services that include providing verified subscribers (tenants) for their property, saving them time and stress in getting tenants. With us, you earn your rental income as and when due without suffering any payment defaults.

How do I schedule to inspect a property?

To schedule a property inspection, you can contact our customer service or visit our website to choose a suitable time. We aim to provide a convenient and flexible experience for our users.

Do I pay for property inspection?

No, property inspections arranged through RentSmallsmall are free of charge for customers.
